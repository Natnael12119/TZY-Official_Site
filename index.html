A Word About Us

TZY Electromechanical and Electrical work contractor were established in 2006 E.C under Ethiopian commercial registration and business license. Proc no k/k AA/14/667/691555/2009 by Ethiopian nationals.

This company founded in Ethiopia by three Electrical Engineers, Those are TEKLAY HAILE, ZEKARIAS DAMTEW and YOHANNES TOLOSSA. All of them have good knowledge and experience on electrical and electromechanical works for instance in power concern works, Telecom project, building electrical installation and design, on different electromechanical works like Air conditioning installation, solar energy, pipe works, structure etc. Our purpose is to be leader in the electromechanical and electrical works by providing enhanced services relationship, profitability and turn knowledge in to value for benefit of our clients, government and people. Our company aims to provide client interest and keep loss of power EEPCO.

Our value determine how we behave, with client and each other, They define what we stand for and how we presume our activities helping us to work together in the most effective and fulfilling way. This enables us to create a strong organization culture constantly throughout the network.
