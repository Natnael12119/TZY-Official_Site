A Word About Us

TZY Electromechanical and Electrical work contractor were established in 2006 E.C under Ethiopian commercial registration and business license. Proc no k/k AA/14/667/691555/2009 by Ethiopian nationals.

This company founded in Ethiopia by three Electrical Engineers, Those are TEKLAY HAILE, ZEKARIAS DAMTEW and YOHANNES TOLOSSA. All of them have good knowledge and experience on electrical and electromechanical works for instance in power concern works, Telecom project, building electrical installation and design, on different electromechanical works like Air conditioning installation, solar energy, pipe works, structure etc. Our purpose is to be leader in the electromechanical and electrical works by providing enhanced services relationship, profitability and turn knowledge in to value for benefit of our clients, government and people. Our company aims to provide client interest and keep loss of power EEPCO.

Our value determine how we behave, with client and each other, They define what we stand for and how we presume our activities helping us to work together in the most effective and fulfilling way. This enables us to create a strong organization culture constantly throughout the network.


What We Do


Mainly we work in 3 areas of practice

Electrical Work

We specialize in a wide range of electrical services, including building electrical installation and design, power concern works, and telecom projects. Our team of experienced electrical engineers is committed to providing high-quality services that meet the needs of our clients. We work closely with our clients to understand their requirements and provide customized solutions that are efficient and cost-effective. From simple electrical repairs to complex installations, we have the expertise to handle it all.

Electro-Mechanical Work

TZY Electromechanical and Electrical Work Contractor also provides a range of electromechanical services, including air conditioning installation, solar energy, pipe works, and structure works. Our team of experts has the knowledge and experience to handle complex projects and ensure that they are completed on time and within budget. We use the latest technology and equipment to ensure that our work is of the highest quality and meets the standards and requirements of our clients.

Network installation

TZY Electromechanical and Electrical Work Contractor also provides network installation services for businesses, government organizations, and residential clients. Our team of professionals has extensive experience in network design, installation, and maintenance, and we use the latest technology and equipment to ensure that our work meets the highest standards of quality and reliability. We work closely with our clients to understand their needs and provide customized solutions that meet their specific requirements.
